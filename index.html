Here's a time-line of Juice_Wrld's life:

  • Dec. 2 1998 - Jarad Higgins widely known as Juice_Wrld was born
  • 2013 - Juice_Wrld battle with drugs began.
  • 2015 - Juice began his career as an independent rapper in Chicago, Illinois
  • 2017 - Juice graduates Homewood-Flossmoor High School and release a three-song EP which was the first album he released.
  • 2018 - Juice started dating Ally Lotti, his soulmate
  • May 23 2018 - Juice released his first studio album "Goodbye and Good Riddance
  • May 9 2019 - Juice released his second studio album "Death Race for Love"
  • Dec. 8 2019 - Higgins suffered a seizure and died on his way to the hospital due to an accidental overdose of codeine and oxycodone
  • Dec. 2 2020 - Ally Lotti reveals that she miscarried Juice's baby due to stress and grief
  • Jan. 12 2022 - Juice_Wrld's mother revealed in the "Tamron Hall Show" that Juice was ready to get help three months before his death.
  • Dec. 13 - Juice_Wrld was buried in Beverly Cemetry, Illinois, United State.
